I am a PhD fellow in Engineering Science at Oslo Metropolitan University, conducting a public sector PhD in collaboration with Statped. My research focuses on the accessibility of digital learning materials for students with visual impairments, exploring how artificial intelligence and universal design principles can bridge the gap between accessibility and pedagogy.
